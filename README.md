# 🚑 HEALER

## Agentic Health Companion for Emergency & Everyday Care

### 👁 See • 🎤 Listen • 🧠 Reason • 🚑 Respond

HEALER is a multilingual, voice-first AI health companion built using Microsoft AI Foundry and Phi-4 Reasoning.

It helps users receive understandable health guidance through text, voice, and image interactions, with a particular focus on emergency first-aid assistance and accessible everyday health support in English, Hindi, and Urdu.

HEALER is designed to assist users when they need immediate guidance, while encouraging consultation with qualified healthcare professionals whenever appropriate.

---

## 🌍 The Problem

Healthcare information is often difficult to access when people need it most.

Many individuals face challenges such as:

* Language barriers
* Low health literacy
* Difficulty understanding medical terminology
* Delayed access to healthcare professionals
* Uncertainty about whether symptoms require urgent attention

These challenges become especially critical during emergencies, where confusion and delayed action can increase risk.

At the same time, many people need trustworthy guidance for common health concerns but may not know whether professional medical attention is necessary.

HEALER was created to make health guidance more accessible, understandable, and actionable.

---

## 💡 What HEALER Does

HEALER combines AI reasoning, multilingual communication, voice interaction, and image analysis into a single experience.

Users can:

* Describe symptoms through text
* Speak naturally using voice input
* Upload images of visible injuries
* Receive step-by-step guidance
* Obtain responses in English, Hindi, or Urdu
* Understand when professional medical care should be sought

---

## 🚨 Emergency Care

Emergency assistance is HEALER's primary focus.

The system is designed to identify potentially serious situations and provide immediate safety-oriented guidance while encouraging professional medical intervention.

### Supported Emergency Scenarios

* Heavy bleeding
* Fractures and injuries
* Road accidents
* Choking
* Burns
* Seizures
* Breathing difficulties
* Loss of consciousness

### Example Workflow

1. User describes an emergency using text, voice, or image.
2. HEALER evaluates the situation.
3. Potential red flags are detected.
4. Urgency is assessed.
5. Immediate stabilization guidance is generated.
6. Emergency escalation recommendations are provided.

### Example

A user reports:

> "There has been a road accident. The person is bleeding heavily and may have a broken leg."

HEALER can:

* Identify the situation as high-risk
* Recommend bleeding control measures
* Suggest safe immobilization practices
* Warn against harmful actions
* Encourage immediate emergency assistance

## 🧠 Why "Agentic"?

Traditional health chatbots primarily answer questions.

HEALER acts more like an assistant that can:

* Listen
* Analyze
* Reason
* Assess urgency
* Generate guidance
* Escalate serious situations

Rather than only returning information, HEALER actively helps users navigate a situation based on the information provided.

## 🩺 WHO-Guided Safety Principles

HEALER uses publicly available health and first-aid guidance informed by World Health Organization (WHO) resources and established emergency response practices.

These resources help guide:

* First-aid recommendations
* Emergency stabilization guidance
* Bleeding control practices
* Choking response guidance
* Safety-oriented escalation decisions

HEALER is not affiliated with or endorsed by the World Health Organization.

---

## 🛡️ Safety & Responsible AI

Safety is a core design principle of HEALER.

The system:

* Does not diagnose diseases
* Does not replace healthcare professionals
* Prioritizes emergency escalation for serious situations
* Encourages users to seek professional medical assistance when necessary
* Focuses on immediate safety-oriented guidance

When severe symptoms or injuries are detected, HEALER prioritizes emergency recommendations and encourages immediate medical attention.

---

## ⚠️ Important Medical Disclaimer

HEALER is an AI-powered health guidance assistant and is **not a replacement for licensed doctors, healthcare professionals, emergency responders, or medical facilities**.

HEALER is intended to:

* Provide health information
* Offer first-aid guidance
* Help users understand urgency
* Encourage timely medical care

HEALER is **not intended to diagnose, prescribe, or replace professional medical judgment**.

For serious symptoms, injuries, ongoing conditions, or life-threatening emergencies, users should always consult a qualified healthcare professional or contact emergency services immediately.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Installation

```bash
git clone <repository-url>
cd healer
npm install
```

### Environment Variables

Create a `.env` file:

```env
AZURE_AI_ENDPOINT=
AZURE_AI_KEY=
AZURE_TRANSLATOR_KEY=
```

### Run

```bash
npm run dev
```
